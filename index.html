The SSB Problem:

In the early 2000's, Trondheim Municipality (along with other Norwegian municipalities), received data from SSB on GHG emissions.

The Project:

The Carbon Track and Trace (CTT) project will develop together with the City of Trondheim (Norway) an automated GHG emissions inventory software system. The CTT project is a partnership between Trondheim Municipality, NTNU Norwegian University of Science and Technology, and ICLEI, and will contribute to mapping out emissions in the building and transport sectors. This timely project comes back to back with local decision makers looking for more accurate ways to measure the impact of Trondheim’s mitigation strategies.
